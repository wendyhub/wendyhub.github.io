Bio

Adele Laurie Blue Adkins is a world renowned English singer-songwriter.
Her first album, 19, was released in 2008 and quickly took the number one spot on the British album chart.
Since then, some of Adele's most popular songs include Someone Like You, Rolling in the Deep, Skyfall, and Hello.
Many of her songs are focused on love and breakups, which she delivers with her powerfully emotional vocals.
